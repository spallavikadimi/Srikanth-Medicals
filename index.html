Frequently Asked Questions

Do you stock prescription medicines? +

Yes, we stock a wide range of prescription medicines from trusted pharmaceutical distributors. Bring or send your prescription and we'll prepare your order.

Can I order through WhatsApp? +

We don't offer home delivery, but you can send your prescription or medicine list to our WhatsApp number to check availability. We'll set it aside for quick pickup at the store.

What are your store timings? +

We're open daily from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM in the morning, and 4:30 PM to 10:30 PM in the evening.

Do you stock baby care products? +

Yes, we carry a range of infant care essentials, baby hygiene products, and nutritional support items for young children.

Do you have healthcare devices? +

Yes, we stock blood pressure monitors, thermometers, glucose meters, pulse oximeters, and other essential healthcare devices.

Are medicines genuine? +

Yes. We source all our medicines from trusted, licensed pharmaceutical distributors to ensure 100% genuine products for our customers.
